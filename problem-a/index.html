Iron Man

Iron Man was a superhero debuted in the film Iron Man directed by Jon Favreau (2 May 2008). Robert Downey Jr. starred this character in this film. The character was depicted as Tony Stark, a wealthy industrialist and ingenious engineer who constructed a powered exoskeleton suit of armor to fight against evil forces. Also, he served as a founding member of the superhero team known as the Avengers and became one of Marvel's most iconic characters in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
